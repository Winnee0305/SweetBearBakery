Decadent Chocolate Tart, Experience the indulgence of our Decadent Chocolate Tart, a rich and velvety dessert that combines a buttery chocolate crust with a smooth chocolate ganache filling.   • Butter: High-quality butter for a tender and flavorful tart crust.
  • Heavy Cream: Brings a luxurious texture to the chocolate ganache.
  • Vanilla Extract: Enhances the overall flavor with a touch of warmth.
