Exquisite Fruit Tart, Indulge in the delightful flavors of our Exquisite Fruit Tart, a perfect harmony of buttery crust, velvety pastry cream, and a vibrant assortment of fresh, seasonal fruits. Each tart is a work of art, meticulously crafted to bring a burst of color and a symphony of flavors to your palate. Whether you choose the mini, normal, or large size, our fruit tarts are a celebration of sweetness and elegance.

  • Butter: Rich and high-quality for a flavorful tart crust.
  • All-Purpose Flour: Creates a tender and buttery tart crust.
  • Pastry Cream: Smooth and luscious cream for the perfect base.
  • Fresh Fruits: A colorful assortment of seasonal fruits for topping.
  • Apricot Glaze: Adds a glossy finish to enhance the tart's appearance.
