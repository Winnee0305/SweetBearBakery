Opera Cake, Indulge in the exquisite layers of our Opera Cake, a French classic that combines the richness of coffee-soaked almond sponge layers with smooth layers of chocolate and coffee-flavored buttercream. The result is a symphony of textures and flavors that dance on your palate, creating a truly luxurious experience. Elevate your dessert affair with the sophistication of our Opera Cake, a perfect blend of artistry and taste.

  • Almond Sponge Layers: Light and airy layers with a hint of almond flavor.
  • Chocolate Ganache: Rich and velvety, adding a decadent chocolate layer.
  • Coffee-Flavored Buttercream: Smooth and flavorful, complementing the almond and chocolate layers.
  • Coffee Syrup: Infused into the almond sponge layers for a delightful coffee essence.
